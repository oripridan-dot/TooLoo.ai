1. Introduction

TooLoo.ai ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Conversations: Any text, conversations, or data you submit to the tool for analysis
  • Feedback: Bug reports, feature requests, or other feedback you provide
  • Optional Data: Email address (if you choose to provide it for notifications)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and general location (country/region level)
  • Timestamps of your interactions
  • Pages or features you access
  • Time spent using the service
  • Referral source (how you found us)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification details (name, email) unless you provide them
  • Payment information (we're not charging during beta)
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Biometric data
  • Health, financial, or sensitive personal data (unless you submit it in conversation)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Service Improvement: Analyzing patterns to improve accuracy and features
  • Research: Understanding how the tool is used and what works best
  • Bug Fixing: Identifying and resolving errors and issues
  • Communication: Responding to your feedback (if you provide contact info)
  • Analytics: Understanding usage patterns (anonymized data)
  • Legal Compliance: Ensuring the service complies with laws

We will NOT use your data to: Sell to third parties, target advertisements, or make automated decisions about you.

4. Data Retention

  • During Beta: Conversations are stored for analysis and improvement
  • After Beta: All data may be deleted 30 days after beta concludes (Nov 15, 2025)
  • Your Request: You can request deletion at any time via the feedback form
  • Legal Hold: We may retain data if required by law or for dispute resolution
  • Logs: Technical logs (IP addresses, timestamps) retained for 90 days

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure data storage
  • Limited access (only necessary team members)
  • Regular backups
  • Incident response procedures

Note: While we use industry-standard protections, no system is 100% secure. By using the service, you assume risk of data transmission.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your conversations and data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable non-essential cookies/tracking
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using the service at any time

To exercise these rights, submit a request through the feedback form in the app.

7. Third-Party Services

  • AI Providers: We use Claude, OpenAI, and other AI services to analyze conversations
  • Data Sharing: Conversations are sent to these providers for analysis only
  • Compliance: These providers have their own privacy policies; we cannot control their practices
  • Your Responsibility: Do not submit sensitive personal data unless you understand these risks

8. Children's Privacy

TooLoo.ai is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Users

  • Other Jurisdictions: Local laws may apply to your data
  • Data Transfers: Your data may be stored outside your country

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy and updating the effective date. Your continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:
